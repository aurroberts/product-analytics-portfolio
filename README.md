# Product Analytics Portfolio

## Overview

This repository documents my transition into Product Analytics through a series of end-to-end business case studies.

Rather than only showcasing my technical skills, each project emphasizes analytical thinking, business reasoning, and data-driven decision making. All investigations begin with a business question, establish clear analytical assumptions, and conclude with actionable recommendations and insights supported by data.

## Project Objective

This portfolio follows the complete analytics workflow:
- Audit and understand the data
- Clean and prepare datasets
- Engineer meaningful features
- Investigate business questions
- Communicate insights through visualizations
- Recommend actions supported by evidence

The first case study analyzes an e-commerce marketplace to understand operational performance, customer satisfaction, and seller performance using SQL, Python, and Tableau.

## Case Study

**Business Context**

An e-commerce marketplace wants to better understand its customer experience and fulfillment operations.

Throughout this portfolio, I investigate business questions related to delivery performance, customer satisfaction, and seller performance while demonstrating an end-to-end analytics workflow.
